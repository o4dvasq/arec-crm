# Avila Real Estate Capital (AREC) — Company Context

## What We Do
Private credit lender exclusively focused on **residential for-sale housing debt**.
- **A&D loans** (80% of capital): unlevered first mortgages to local developers on fully entitled land with public builder lot contracts
- **Vertical construction loans** (20% of capital): leveraged construction loans to private builders (rank 40–200 nationally, 200–1,000 homes/year)
- **No build-to-rent** — exclusively owner-occupied housing
- **No fund-level leverage** on A&D loans; modest leverage (68-70%) only on vertical side

## Business Lines
1. **Avila Real Estate Capital** — the fund (Tony: 80% of time)
2. **Builder Advisor Group (BAG)** — registered broker-dealer for M&A advisory and capital raising for private home builders. 100+ transactions since 1996.

California (3), Colorado, Texas (multiple: Dallas, Houston, Lubbock), Georgia (Atlanta), Pennsylvania, New Jersey, New York, New Mexico, Orlando FL

## Strategy Differentiators
- Pre-sold lots before loan funding (hard deposits from builders)
- In-house line-item development cost underwriting
- Developers/builders as LPs = perfect information + alignment
- 120% release rate for ongoing deleveraging
- Can pause construction starts on one day's notice
- Full data room transparency for LPs
